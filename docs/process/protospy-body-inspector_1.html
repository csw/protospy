protospyv0.1.0-dev
Design decisions: The list is narrow (260px) — just enough to distinguish requests by path, method, status, and req/res size. The body indicator in each list item (e.g. req 1.8K / res 12.4K) helps you know before clicking whether there's something worth reading. Query params are broken out into a structured key-value strip above the bodies rather than buried in the URL — for Elasticsearch, filter_path, routing, and pretty are semantically meaningful and deserve readable display. The two body panes are equal-width — both readable at around 80 columns of monospace. The request body has a highlight band on lines 5–12 showing the multi_match clause, which you'd get from clicking a fold marker or from a search. The response pane uses inline fold-hints (… 4 keys, … 16 more hits) to keep the structure navigable without hiding content entirely. What's intentionally absent: headers (in a collapsed drawer, accessible but not defaulting to screen real estate), timing, trace linkage. Those live in the other modes reachable from the top-right tab strip.
